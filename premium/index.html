Get Gmelius Premium

Gmelius Premium (Beta) lets you play with the newest Gmelius features. Let us know what you think and do not hesitate to share your ideas. By downloading the Premium version, you'll always get the latest performance and feature updates. Note - though - that Gmelius Premium is still in Beta, implying that some of its functionalities can be a little rough around the edges. Ensure to deactivate any previous copy of Gmelius from your browser after installing Gmelius Premium in order to avoid any conflicts. Gmelius Premium is currently free while in Beta but will offer paid premium features in the future, stay tuned.

Gmelius is "honor-ware", which means that we trust each other to be nice. We are committed to propose a software of quality by releasing new features and correcting possible bugs on a constant basis but we need your support. Give a try to Gmelius, and if you intend to keep it, ensure to donate a small amount.

As a user, please note that it's your responsibilty to ensure that you always run the latest version of the extension. Updates are announced via Google+, Facebook and Twitter, and it is highly recommended to follow Gmelius on either of these networks to be kept updated.
